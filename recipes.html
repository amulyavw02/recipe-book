Kidney Bean Salad with Cream of Mushroom on side

Ingredients:

  • Kidney beans - 2 cups, cooked
  • Onions, carrots, peppers - diced
  • Cream sauce - 1 cup
  • Basic spices
  • Cream of mushroom

Instructions:

  1. Sauté vegetables until soft
  2. Add beans and cream sauce
  3. Simmer for 10 minutes
  4. Season and serve hot

Aglio e Olio

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti - 250g
  • Garlic - 6 cloves, sliced and Red chili flakes - 1 tsp
  • Parsley - 2 tbsp, chopped and Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Sauté garlic in olive oil until golden
  3. Add chili flakes and cooked pasta
  4. Toss with parsley, salt, and pepper

Spinach rice with yogurt curry

Ingredients:

  • Green spinach paste
  • Yogurt with masalas and spices
  • 2 cloves garlic and onions, minced
  • 1 cup rice

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice with added green color
  2. Simmer curry paste with yogurt
  3. Stir in chili powder, cumin, and tomato sauce. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve curry around green rice

Thai curry with rice

Ingredients:

  • Green curry paste - 2 tbsp
  • Coconut milk - 1 can
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Rice - 2 cups

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice
  2. Simmer curry paste with coconut milk
  3. Add vegetables and cook
  4. Serve green curry with rice

Chocolate Mug Cake

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, mix flour, sugar, and cocoa powder.
  2. Add egg and mix well. Stir in milk, oil, and vanilla extract.
  3. Microwave on high for 1-2 minutes, or until the cake is cooked through.
